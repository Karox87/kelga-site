Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Kelga. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our poultry farm management application.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (username, password, email address)
  • Farm business data (inventory, sales records, expenses)
  • Employee information (names, roles, salary data)
  • Financial transactions and accounting records
  • Device information for app optimization

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Provide and maintain our farm management services
  • Process your business transactions and generate reports
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Ensure data security and prevent unauthorized access
  • Provide customer support when requested

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission and storage
  • Secure authentication with automatic lockout protection
  • Role-based access control for multi-user environments
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Your farm data remains confidential and is only accessible to authorized users within your organization. We may share information only when required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us directly.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal data
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your business data in standard formats
  • Opt-out of any optional data collection

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by updating the effective date below and through in-app notifications for significant changes.
